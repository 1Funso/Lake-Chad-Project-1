Wildlife

Lake Chad is home to diverse wildlife, including birds, fish, and mammals. The lake provides critical habitat for many species and serves as an essential breeding ground and stopover site for migratory birds.

Tourism

Tourism plays a vital role in the local economy and helps raise awareness about the importance of preserving Lake Chad's unique ecosystem. Visitors can enjoy activities such as bird watching, fishing, and cultural experiences with local communities.
