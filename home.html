Welcome to Lake Chad Conservation Project

Lake Chad is a historically large, shallow, endorheic lake in Africa that has experienced fluctuations in size throughout centuries. It serves as a crucial source of fresh water and sustains the livelihoods of millions of people in the surrounding regions, including Nigeria, Chad, Cameroon, and Niger.

Unfortunately, the lake has been shrinking rapidly in recent decades due to climate change and human activities. This ecological imbalance has significantly affected the diverse wildlife that calls Lake Chad home. From majestic elephants to graceful deers, Lake Chad is home to a remarkable array of animal species.

To protect and preserve this precious ecosystem, the Lake Chad Conservation Project is dedicated to implementing sustainable conservation efforts. Our mission is to restore the lake's ecological balance and safeguard the habitats of these incredible animal varieties.

We invite you to explore the breathtaking beauty of Lake Chad and the remarkable diversity of its wildlife through stunning pictures capturing the essence of this unique ecosystem. Browse through our gallery of captivating animal varieties. These images offer a glimpse into the awe-inspiring world of Lake Chad's wildlife and the urgent need to protect their habitats.

We invite you to explore more about our ongoing efforts, the rich history of Lake Chad, and discover how you can contribute to our cause. Together, we can make a positive impact and safeguard Lake Chad for future generations.
